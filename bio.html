BRAD MALLETT

I have been an artist for as long as I can remember. It started as a competition between my twin brother and I. We would draw cityscapes, cartoons and rollercoasters, comparing our final drawings. Stemming from this competitive foundation, I found a way of expressing myself. I like exploring with the way light and color interact with imagninative shapes. At 29 and recovering from Lyme Disease, I feel it's time to push the envelope.
