BRAD MALLETT

My work falters between the representational and the abstract. In my work latest work, I try to display color in unique interactions not seen often. I view the structure, the form and composition, as the stage. The colors are the real actors. They tug and pull and talk to each other. I try to find the perfect balance of struggle and agreement.

I'm from Iowa. I'm a twin. I'm 29. I try and follow a faithful God, and currently I'm enthralled in astronomy and cosmology.
